The Monty Hall Problem

This is the Monty Hall problem, made famous by Monty Hall on the gameshow, "Let's Make a Deal"

Once you click start, there will be multiple doors for you to click on. Behind one of the doors is a prize, and behind the rest of the doors there is nothing. After choosing your door, we will show you one door that has nothing behind it. After that, you will have the opportunity to switch your selection to a new door. Good luck!

Scoring:
+1 for correct guesses, -1 for incorrect guesses
